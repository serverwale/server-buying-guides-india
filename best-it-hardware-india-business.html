1. Rack servers for core infrastructure

For virtualization, databases, ERP and application hosting, refurbished enterprise rack servers give you the best value. A Dell PowerEdge R740 or an HPE ProLiant DL380 Gen10 delivers data-centre-grade reliability at 50-70% below new pricing. When you need bulk capacity rather than bleeding-edge speed — file servers, backups, media libraries, surveillance — refurbished SAN and NAS storage arrays and high-bay servers like the Dell R740xd are the economical choice. Buy what you need now and expand drives later.
