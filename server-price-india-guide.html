How Much Does a Server Cost in India? (2026 Price Guide)

Server pricing depends on CPU, RAM, storage and whether it is refurbished or new. Here are honest indicative ranges from Serverwale for India.

Indicative refurbished price ranges

  • Entry 1U/2U dual-Xeon: ₹35,000–60,000
  • Mid virtualization host (256–512GB RAM): ₹80,000–1,80,000
  • GPU server (single GPU): from ₹1,50,000
  • Multi-GPU AI node: several lakh, by configuration

Pricing varies with generation, RAM, drives and GPUs — always confirm the exact config.

What drives the price?

CPU generation and core count, total RAM, number and type of drives (SSD vs HDD, NVMe), RAID controller, and GPUs are the biggest factors. Warranty and support also matter for total value.
